01 / PROFILE

Engineering solutions where physics meets practice.

I am a fluid-mechanics researcher focused on hydrogen combustion, turbulence and industrial safety. My research combines numerical modelling with experimental evidence to understand flame acceleration, overpressure and transient flow in complex geometries.

Before joining IIT Madras, I spent more than six years working in plant engineering at Tata Steel. I contributed to equipment maintenance, commissioning and drawing-based plant modifications with a strong focus on reliability, maintainability and safety.

This combination allows me to examine engineering problems from both perspectives: the governing physics and the practical realities of industrial equipment.
