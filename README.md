## About Me

I am an MS (Research) scholar in Applied Mechanics at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, specializing in computational fluid dynamics, hydrogen combustion, turbulence and engineering simulation.

Before joining IIT Madras, I gained more than six years of industrial engineering experience at Tata Steel, working on equipment maintenance, commissioning, engineering drawings, P&IDs and plant modifications.

## Featured Work

### Hydrogen Explosion Dynamics

CFD–experiment validation of hydrogen deflagration in complex and congested geometries using a hydrogen-capable PDRFoam workflow.

### SEA-RAFT Flow Diagnostics

Application of optical flow and computer vision to extract transient velocity information from combustion imaging.

### Industrial Engineering

Drawing-based equipment modification, commissioning, maintenance and reliability work performed at Tata Steel.
